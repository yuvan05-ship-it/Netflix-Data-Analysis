# TOPIC 📊 Netflix Data Analysis
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset to uncover trends, patterns, and insights using Python.
> Turning raw Netflix data into meaningful insights about global content trends.

## 📖 Project Story

With the rapid growth of streaming platforms like Netflix, understanding content trends has become essential to identify what drives audience engagement and platform expansion.

In this project, I explored Netflix’s dataset to answer key questions such as:
- What type of content dominates the platform?
- How has Netflix’s content library grown over time?
- Which countries contribute the most content?
- What trends can be observed in ratings and genres?

Through data cleaning and exploratory data analysis using Python, I uncovered patterns in content distribution, growth trends, and global production dominance.

The analysis revealed that Movies form the majority of Netflix’s catalog, with the United States leading in content production. Additionally, a significant surge in content addition was observed after 2015, indicating Netflix’s rapid expansion phase.

This project demonstrates how data analysis can transform raw datasets into meaningful insights, helping understand real-world business strategies in the streaming industry.

## 💼 Business Understanding
This analysis goes beyond basic data exploration by providing insights into Netflix’s content strategy and business growth.

- 📌 The dominance of Movies over TV Shows suggests Netflix initially focused on scalable content to quickly expand its library.
- 📌 The United States being the top content producer highlights Netflix’s strong base market and content sourcing strategy.
- 📌 The sharp increase in content after 2015 reflects Netflix’s aggressive global expansion and investment in original content.
- 📌 The gradual rise in TV Shows indicates a shift toward long-form content to improve user engagement and retention.

These insights help understand how Netflix leverages content to attract and retain subscribers, expand globally, and compete in the streaming industry.
